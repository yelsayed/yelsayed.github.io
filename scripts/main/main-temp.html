About me

Hi my name is Yasser El-Sayed, I'm 20 years old. In short, I am Computer Science student at CMU that is extremely passionate about most fields in Computer Science. I am a hardcore, Full Stack Developer . Put me in pretty much any field and I'll blossom. My main passion, however, is Front End Web development and Architecture Design . You'll find that I am familiar with a lot of Front End tools. I can build animations, components, and a structurally sound Front End code base . I am also passionate about core Systems Programming and have a very good understanding about Network and Distributed System design principles. On top of that, I've delved very deeply into Network Programming and have made concrete networking systems that required significant design and engineering. For my free time I like writing. I've written many short stories that reside in this website's articles section. Also I like to read about world issues and blog from unique people to enrich my view on this earth.
